Evil Martians

Design: This website is filled with a number of notable design features including clean animations and clean, flowing transitions as you scroll through the page.

Usability: While very usable and easy to follow for most audiences, some people may struggle with perhaps not being informed to scroll down for more info on the main page, or they may find the extra animations distracting and confusing.

Content: There is a lot of content regarding this organization that is clearly displayed on the left hand side the entire time. The ‘contact us’ button is also clearly visible at most times.

Creativity: This website is quite creative in its design; while maintaining a clear format showing its content at all times. It’s home page especially features a wonderful composition and movable objects that add a fun element to the page.
