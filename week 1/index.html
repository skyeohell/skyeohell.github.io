Review 1: Vermillion Jewelry

Vermillion Jewelry

Design: This website is very creative with its use of design elements, featuring a creative scrolling feature that cleanly leads you through the home page.

Usability: This website is usable for most audiences as it features many of its primary features just through scrolling down the main page. Its unique menu options could confuse some.

Content: With features such as unique content including a gallery and a journal section, it holds even more information in subsections for some menu options, offering information about the company, its products, and its goals.

Creativity: With a very creative display, the website features unique content and showcases it in an upfront yet satisfying way.
